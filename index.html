About

Systems-first, performance-aware, and happiest at the hardware-software boundary.

I’m drawn to work where architecture decisions matter: chips, runtimes, kernels, toolchains, and the engineering details that turn ambitious ideas into fast, reliable systems.

My background spans FPGA projects, RISC-V processor design, C/C++ systems coursework, and practical experimentation with ML and inference-oriented infrastructure. I care about clarity as much as capability, so I like building things that are both technically sharp and easy to explain.
